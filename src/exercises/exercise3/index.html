Customizable Product Card

Create a dynamic, interactive product card where users can customize product options like color and size, and see real-time updates on the card.

  • Design a product card that includes:
    • A product image.
    • A product title/name.
    • A price that updates based on selected options.
    • Dropdowns or buttons for selecting options such as color and size.
  • When a user selects different options (e.g., color, size), the product image and price should dynamically update to reflect their choices.
  • Implement responsive design so that the product card looks good on both desktop and mobile screens.
